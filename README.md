# Superstore Sales & Risk Analysis Dashboard

## Objective
The objective of this project is to analyze overall sales performance, evaluate customer profitability, and identify loss-making areas to understand business risk and revenue concentration.

---

## Dashboards Included

- **Sales & Profit Performance Dashboard**  
  Shows overall sales, profit trends, and regional performance.

- **Loss & Risk Analysis Dashboard**  
  Highlights customer-level losses, risky segments, and profit concentration.

---

## Key Insights

- The **West region** contributes the **highest share of overall profit**.  
- The **East region** records the **highest customer-level losses**.  
- The **Consumer segment** dominates total sales volume.  
- A **small group of customers drives most of the profit**, indicating **risk concentration**.

---
